While sailing with my friend Tom we were passed up by _America_, which is a replica of [America](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/America_(yacht)>), the original winner of the America’s cup. This was a few miles off of Point Loma outside of San Diego Bay.

![](https://images.kenchinpurcell.com/america-4e5a96809c.jpg "America")

She was flying along at around 10-12 knots, easily outpacing us, and she didn’t even have all her sails up.
